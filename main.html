Paris


Paris, the city of lights, is famous for its art, fashion, and culture.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - Iconic landmark of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum with its Mona Lisa
  3. Notre Dame Cathedral
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica
  5. Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants and pastries
  • Baguettes fresh from the bakery
  • Escargots (snails)
  • French Cheese varieties
  • Crème Brûlée dessert
