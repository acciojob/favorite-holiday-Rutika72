Paris, France


Paris, known as the "City of Light", is famous for its romantic ambiance, rich history, and iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum. It's a destination that offers art, culture, and exquisite cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The symbol of Paris with breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – Famous for the artistic vibe and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Iconic avenue with shopping and cafes.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky and buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate almond-based sweets.
  • Escargots – A French delicacy of snails cooked in garlic butter.
  • Crepes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Ratatouille – Traditional vegetable stew full of flavors.
